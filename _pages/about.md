Seyed Milad Hatamlee
Ph.D. Candidate | Mechanical Engineering | University at Buffalo

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at the University at Buffalo. My research focuses on molecular simulations of fluid transport and phase behavior in nanoconfined systems, with applications in CO₂ transport, water flow in nanochannels, and energy-related porous media.

My work combines molecular dynamics, Monte Carlo simulation, and thermodynamic modeling to better understand transport and equilibrium behavior at the nanoscale.


**Research Interests:** Molecular Dynamics, Monte Carlo Simulation, Nanoconfined Fluids, CO₂ Transport, Phase Equilibrium, Energy Systems, Multiphase Transport
