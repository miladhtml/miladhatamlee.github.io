## Overview

This research focuses on understanding thermal transport and mechanical behavior in two-dimensional (2D) materials using molecular dynamics simulations. The work explores how atomic structure, bonding, and interfaces influence material properties at the nanoscale, with applications in nanoelectronics, thermal management, and advanced materials design.

---

## Thermal Transport

### Graphene Polymorphs and Carbon-Based Structures

- Investigated thermal conductivity across various graphene-derived lattices  
- Identified strong dependence of thermal transport on lattice structure and bonding  
- Established structure–property relationships governing heat conduction  

![Graphene structures](../images/graphene_structures.png)

*Representative graphene-based structures used for thermal transport analysis.*

---

### Interfacial Thermal Conductance (ITR) in 2D Heterostructures

- Studied heat transfer across interfaces between dissimilar 2D materials  
- Quantified interfacial thermal resistance using molecular dynamics simulations  
- Analyzed the role of lattice mismatch and vibrational coupling  

![Interface heat transfer](../images/interface_itr.TIF)

*Illustration of heat transfer across a 2D material interface.*

## Mechanical Behavior

### MXene Structures

- Modeled mechanical properties of MXene materials under deformation  
- Investigated stress–strain response and structural stability  
- Identified key parameters controlling mechanical strength  

![MXene structure](../images/mxene_structure.png)

*Atomic structure of MXene materials used in simulations.*

---

### 2D Black Phosphorene

- Studied anisotropic mechanical behavior using molecular dynamics  
- Analyzed tensile and shear response along different crystallographic directions  
- Identified directional dependence of strength and deformation mechanisms  

![Phosphorene structure](../images/phos_final_tensile.mp4)
![Phosphorene structure](../images/phoss_final_shear.mp4)

## Key Contributions

- Developed molecular-level understanding of heat transport in low-dimensional systems  
- Established relationships between structure and thermal conductivity  
- Advanced modeling of interfacial thermal transport in 2D materials  
- Characterized anisotropic mechanical behavior in emerging nanomaterials
